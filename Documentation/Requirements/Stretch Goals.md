* **Acceptance Criteria:**

* Students receive a notification when they receive a new card.
* Students receive a notification when an instructor approves / denies a card they sent.
* Students receive a notification when an instructor approves / denies a class join-request.
* Instructors receive a notification when a student requests to join a class they manage.
* Instructors receive a reminder notification to review cards when they have 5, 10, 15, . . . unreviewed cards.
